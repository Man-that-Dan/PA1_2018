This was a program intended to remove noise from a series of photos and produce one clean photo
or remove an unwanted object from a series of photos.


Usage: 
	Unzip image files or provide your own, program expects 10 images for average method
	and 9 for median.
	Use makefile to compile program. 
	Use format: ./noise <reduction type> <output file>
    Example usage: ./noise median mediantest.ppm
